ABOUT POCKET_PHP

The original idea was to fix some of PHP annoyances that slow down development without having to sacrifice performance by using one of the many web frameworks already available. Since the aim was to speed up development, a template containing the smallest amount of cookie cutter code was incorporated and it became a starting point for many of my PHP projects. I keep it as simple as possible, only the very essential is provided so the developer can get straight to work on the application.

POCKET_PHP is developed by AlexHG on his free time.



WHO IS IT FOR?

Anyone looking to take advantage of PHP's performance and simplicity without having to depend on a bloated and complicated framework that removes power from the programmer in exchange of utility that the developer can implement without much effort in the first place. The framework was built with NGINX in mind but any webserver will do, simply replicate the annotations in the provided configuration.

Due to how easy it is to setup and how thorough the configuration is, I personally use it to serve anonymous TOR websites and quick prototypes.
